About Me

My name is Lingfei Wu , I am a post-doctoral researcher at the Center for the Study of Institutional Diversity at Arizona State University. For more information please find my CV.

Research Interest

I study the dynamics of social attention online using network models: how collective attention is transported between information resources and how this transport of attention shapes the evolution of online information systems. You are welcomed to visit my blog .
